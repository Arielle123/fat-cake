Delicious Fat Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons yeast
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups warm water
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add warm water and mix into a soft dough.
  3. Cover and let rise for 1 hour.
  4. Heat oil and fry spoonfuls of dough until golden brown.
  5. Drain on paper towels and enjoy!
